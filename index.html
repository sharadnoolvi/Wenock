About WeNock

How it all started

We have noticed a significant rise in the number of home chefs, bakers and small-sized caterers who have turned their passion into business opportunities from the last few years. The previous two years of the pandemic are another reason triggering this uptake in many self-employed home chefs. Based on our experience, these home cooks are in no way less than your high street restaurants compared to professionalism, taste and quality. We see consumers being part of multiple WhatsApp groups ordering food from this bunch of talented cooks and bakers. However, we believe a full-fledged marketplace connecting both consumers and home chefs is missing, as it's not the case with other known food outlets on the high street that are being taken care of by Deliveroo, JustEat, UberEats, etc.

We founded WeNock as a one-stop shop to provide a unique, practical and convenient solution for consumers who love to order authentic home-cooked food from their favourite home chefs and local caterers part of their neighbourhood. Our easy-to-use food delivery app allows food lovers to choose from an array of home chefs and caterers in their community and pre-order their meal at their convenience, which is delivered free* to their doorsteps. Using our App, customers can select different cuisines and dishes from multiple caterers of their liking and have them delivered as part of one single order. Customers now don't have to go through the hassle of constantly monitoring their WhatsApp groups to see which home-chef has published what menu for the week and order them from different google forms. Instead, they can get notified and order through the WeNock App!



Our Team

We are an exciting team of five-member technology enthusiasts who also love authentic home-cooked food. Having understood and experienced the challenges of being a customer and a home chef, we want to introduce an entirely new Digital Experience in this unknown and unexplored segment of home cooks in the UK. We are aware that this journey will sometimes hurl unpleasant surprises at us. However, with passion and the use of the right technology, we can overcome these hurdles. And we shall strive hard to keep the overall experience for both customers and caterers consistent and satisfactory.

Create-Change-Publish your Menu as you prefer Manage day-wise Menu round the week Maintain and re-use your Menu templates we understand that Menu Management is one of the most vital tasks, and we make this process convenient for you through our Platform. Seamless Order Management

Devote your valuable time creating delicious dishes-leave the mundane & time-consuming work of order management to WeNock

Plan your inventory with the help of WeNock's cat360 dashboard. We shall give you a comprehensive view of your orders a day in advance so that you can plan on your inventory and avoid any unpleasant surprises on the day of your cooking



Marketing and loyalty Support

Transparent & Secure Payment Management

The Platform offers your customers a seamless digital payment system using all kinds of payment methods such as credit, debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal.

You don't have to receive payments directly into your bank account from customers in the future. WeNock shall consolidate all the customer payments and credit the sum to your business account and provide a complete report against the settlement made

The caterer can raise manual settlement requests if any ad-hoc payment is to be made from WeNock.

The Platform will provide a complete view of revenue generated for the orders delivered by the caterer on a daily, weekly and monthly basis.
