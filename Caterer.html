We want to grow with you

WeNock is a budding start-up mainly focusing on delivering food to the neighbourhood, home-cooked by passionate chefs. We have noticed a significant rise in the number of home chefs, bakers and small-sized caterers who have turned their passion into business opportunities from the last few years. The previous two years of the pandemic are another reason triggering this uptake in many self-employed home chefs. Based on our experience, these home cooks are in no way less than your high street restaurants compared to professionalism, taste and quality. Our motive is to provide that necessary technical and delivery infrastructure to these upcoming and passionate home chefs to run their businesses smoothly and grow along with them.

Flexible Menu Management

Create-Change-Publish your Menu as you prefer Manage day-wise Menu round the week Maintain and re-use your Menu templates we understand that Menu Management is one of the most vital tasks, and we make this process convenient for you through our Platform. Seamless Order Management

Devote your valuable time creating delicious dishes-leave the mundane & time-consuming work of order management to WeNock

Once you have completed packing your orders for the day or a batch, WeNock's delivery person will be at your doorsteps to collect and deliver the orders with a click of a button. And you can track the orders to completion

Plan your inventory with the help of WeNock's cat360 dashboard. We shall give you a comprehensive view of your orders a day in advance so that you can plan on your inventory and avoid any unpleasant surprises on the day of your cooking





Marketing and loyalty Support

Through our Platform, you can launch attractive offers to the customers.

Run campaigns during festive seasons and offer discounts on specific Menus or combination of dishes

Transparent & Secure Payment Management

The Platform offers your customers a seamless digital payment system using all kinds of payment methods such as credit, debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal.

You don't have to receive payments directly into your bank account from customers in the future. WeNock shall consolidate all the customer payments and credit the sum to your business account and provide a complete report against the settlement made

The caterer can raise manual settlement requests if any ad-hoc payment is to be made from WeNock.

The Platform will provide a complete view of revenue generated for the orders delivered by the caterer on a daily, weekly and monthly basis.






FAQ



How do Rewards work?

WeNock will reward users when shared a reward code to signup for non WeNock users.

How do I refer a Friend, what benefit do I get?

Just log in to WeNock App and then select Reward & Earn there will be a unique code available to share with your friends and family.

Does the WeNock app work both in iOS/Android?

Our App will work on Apple iOS Mobiles as well as most of Android devices.



How do you use my Favourite, Preferences, Allergy Informations?

Under the profile section, favourite food and other preference can be set. Allergy information is available at every dish level.

Is my payment detail secure in the WeNock portal?

WeNock is using industry standard gateway for payment process and there for it is secure.

How does WeNock Wallet work?

Wallet within WeNock app can be topped up by additional money as well as all rewards will get converted to wallet money to spend on WeNock App.



FAQ

Why do I need to use my mobile for registration instead of email?

WeNock is a Passwordless system and uses more secure Code base authentication.

What is the cut off for Pre-Ordering the Menu?

24 hours before delivery is mostly set by caterers as Cut-Off time for orders.

Up to what time/date can I cancel my Pre-Order

24 hours before delivery order can be cancelled.

Can I cancel part of my order or from one caterer and retain other caterer orders?

It’s not possible to cancel part of the order.

How long does the Refund processing take?

Refunds for cancelled orders will take 7-10 working days.
